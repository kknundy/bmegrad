About BME Graduate Club

The NUS BME Graduate Students' Club was formed in May 2009 by graduate students from what was then known as the Division of Bioengineering to enhance the stay of Biomedical Engineering graduate students during the course of our graduate studies here in NUS.

About the logo

This logo has been brought to life by the Executive committee of 2010/2011

  • “Bright Innovative Engineers” who have coalesced biology with engineering represent the letters “B I E” in the logo.
  • The DNA symbolizes “Bright life” in the NUS graduate student community.
  • The Axel in the logo, represents the NUS engineering community. Innovations in engineering have revolutionized the approach to biological sciences.
  • The Graduate hat represents every graduate student in the Division of Bioengineering with a ‘Tie’ depicting professionalism, inculcating good networking abilities for intra-departmental student interactions.
